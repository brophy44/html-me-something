Concerts of 2016

  • Dave Matthews Band at Atlanta, GA
  • Journey at St. Louis, MO
  • Lindi Ortega at Nashville, TN

Dave Matthews Band in Atlanta, GA

My parents and siblings attended a DMB concert in May 2016 at Atlanta's Ampitheatre. We arrived to talegate in the city at 5pm. Once parked we enjoyed sandwiches, fruit, desserts and cold drinks.
The concert started at 8pm. We were seated at 7pm and were lucky to have backstage passes where we could enjoy a closer view of the stage. Dave played his hit songs including "Grey Street", "So Much to Say", and "Crash Into Me" during his set.

Journey in St. Louis, MO

My boyfriend Thomas, myself and our friends enjoyed the Journey concert at the STL Ampitheatre. We enjoyed sandwiches before the concert at the lawn seats with our blankets.
During the show, Journey played some of their classic songs including "Anyway You Want It", "Open Arms", and "Faithfully". They ended their show with their most popular song, "Don't Stop Believing".

Lindi Ortega in Nashville, TN

Thomas, my dad, my sister and me traveled to Nashville to visit their music scene. Their we enjoyed various shows around the city, trying different foods and refreshments but Lindi Ortega's concert was the main event. She performed at the City Winery.
